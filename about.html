OVERVIEW

Welcome to EYEZONE – your resource in eye health essentials, esteemed by vision specialists worldwide.

A vision in sight

EYEZONE is a premiere international magazine established with an eye to enrich the optical trade and eye care publication in the Middle East and across the globe. Since its launch in 2005, EYEZONE recapitulated a vision, not only in groundbreaking the first Arab optical magazine, but also in affording continuing education to eye care professionals. With its clear-cut outlook and dedication to offer ocular essentials to its subscribers, the EYEZONE team resolved “to contribute to society’s growth” through specialized training in optometric science.

A spectacle of success

EYEZONE sought from the outset to impart a balanced variety of topics, from ocular health to digital lenses, and from eyewear psychology to vision guides. It serves as a lens for readers interested in new perspectives from the field of optical studies. EYEZONE and its parent company, Mediazone, have a spectrum of editorial coverage in nearly 20 countries throughout the Middle East, North Africa, including Iran and Turkey. More than 8,000 copies are distributed once every other month by FedEx to retail optical stores and eye clinics throughout the region. A Persian language section is also supplemented to its bi-monthly issues. Moreover, the magazine likewise features a Fashion Supplement that exhibits an assemblage of leading eyewear designers.
